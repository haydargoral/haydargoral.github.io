Mathematician

haydargoral@iyte.edu.tr
Izmir, Turkey

I am a mathematician with a strong interest in Number Theory, Model Theory, and their applications to Diophantine Geometry. My research focuses on arithmetic progressions, Szemerédi's theorem, prime numbers, zeta functions, finite fields, combinatorics on words, and theoretical computer science. I am also interested in exploring the intersection of mathematics and machine learning techniques.
